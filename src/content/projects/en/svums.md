Joining a club usually means a paper form, a back and forth over missing fields, and someone typing it all into a spreadsheet later. This replaces that with a single online form that produces clean records from the start.

### What it does

- Lets prospective members apply online through a guided web form.
- Validates the input so the club receives complete, structured data.
- Hands the committee a record they can act on instead of a scan to retype.

### Why it matters

It removes a small but recurring chore from the people who run the club, and it gives new members a smoother first interaction than a PDF to print and post.
