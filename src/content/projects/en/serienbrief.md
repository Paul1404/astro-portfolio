Clubs keep member records that slowly go out of date, and chasing corrections by hand is thankless work. This generates personalised letters that let members fix their own data.

### What it does

- Imports member data and generates personalised mail-merge letters as PDFs.
- Prints a QR code on each letter that takes the member to a self-service portal.
- Members update their own details, so the records correct themselves.

### Why it matters

It turns the maintenance of a club's member database from a manual chase into a loop members complete on their own. Built for and used by a real organisation.
