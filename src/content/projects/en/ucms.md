Plenty of small organisations need a website and have no one to build or maintain one. ucms gives them a visual editor and hosting for several sites on a single instance, so a volunteer can put a site together and keep it current.

### What it does

- Offers a drag-and-drop editor with ready-made section types: hero, gallery, contact, FAQ, map, and more.
- Hosts multiple sites on one instance, each with its own domain slug and assigned editors.
- Handles drafts and publishing with autosave, undo, SEO metadata, and optional S3 image storage.

### Why it matters

It puts a maintainable website within reach of people who would otherwise go without one, and keeps the whole thing on infrastructure they can host themselves.
